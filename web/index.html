RELEAF WEB REDESIGN

I redesigned the web interface for Releaf, a data company dedicated to connecting investors with small farmers. After researching business profiles of competitors, I noticed that most interfaces were cluttered and often hard to understand, from a technical perspective. My client wanted the application to be accessible even to people unfamiliar with technicalities, so I kept the design simple, friendly and accessible.
