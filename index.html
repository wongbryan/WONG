RELEAF REDESIGN

I redesigned the online business profile interface for ReLeaf, a data company created to help connect investors and local businesses in Nigeria, and beyond. When researching other data company websites, we noticed that the layout was very static and uninviting to the user. Although this made for a more traditional/professional feel, the interface often felt cluttered, unfocused and not user-friendly. The objective for the ReLeaf redesign was to simplify all the technicalities and business-related jargon so that relatively inexperienced users could still find useful investments. Full designs can be viewed here.

MUSIC

I made MUSIC as a brief homage to two of my favorite musical artists, Joji and Kevin Abstract. I experimented with SVG clip-paths with videos, stroke animations and basic object-oriented programming. Made with vanilla Javascript/CSS. MUSIC can be viewed here.

PINEAPPLES

Pineapples is a short web project to showcase two instances of the portrayal of pineapples in media: ChungKing Express (dir. Wong Kar Wai) and Pineapple Tours, an indie rock band. Made with vanilla Javascript/CSS. Pineapples can be viewed here.

A DAY IN LA

I am a developer on UCLA Creative Lab's "A Day in LA" interactive narrative. The idea is to feature quirks, unique sights and information about Los Angeles in a way that mocks Angelino culture but also gives it novelty. I handled the made the SVG graphics/animations and created a framework to handle loading/transitioning between scenes. The project is a work in progress. A Day in LA can be viewed here.
